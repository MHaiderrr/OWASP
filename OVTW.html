Welcome to OWASP Vulnerability Testing

This website serves as a comprehensive resource for understanding the top vulnerabilities outlined by OWASP. Our goal is to educate developers, security professionals, and organizations about these vulnerabilities and provide guidance on how to mitigate them effectively.

Explore our resources to learn about each vulnerability in detail, access testing tools, and improve your security posture.
